Abacus, the technical exhibitory event of computer engineering department is one of the most prestigious event which takes place in Vidyavardhini's College of engineering and technology. Students come with their own set of technical skills and showcase their projects before the faculty members and fellow students. Every year, Abacus comes with its unique theme which reflects the modern day advancement in the computer field which attracts a huge number of students. Abacus aims to guide young engineers in such efficient manner that the innovators in them take life again. This year, Abacus has ventured into the product showcase which enhances students' knowledge about current trends in the computer world and introduces them to the real time industry related products. The event is judged by experienced industry professionals who encourage  students with their remarkable and valuable feedback. Bringing in the products, helps the students to fill the technological gap between their projects and real time products.
