YANTRA.

Department of Instrumentation Engineering

"YANTRA-TECHNICAL PRODUCT EXHIBITION" is organized by VCET ISA (International Society of Automation) of Instrumentation Department. YANTRA is one of the fabulous event which takes place at Vidyavardhini's College of Engineering and Technology (VCET). In this technical event we approach Electrical and Instrumentation companies to showcase their products in our campus so that students from various diploma & degree engineering colleges visit the exhibition and gain a great deal ofÂ  knowledge of the real world. Every year we invite more number of companies, so that the students can gain knowledge of Instrumentation. YANTRA is not only about product exhibition but it is an opportunity for students to interact with various companies. This exhibition is a great learning experience for our students as they get to interact with the industry and explain the products themselves. The event is judged by experienced company staff who encourages students with remarkable feedback. The event witnesses an attendance of close to 1000-1500 college students from across the city. The student will be acquainted with the products that the company manufacture.

ANVESHAN.

Department of Electronics & Telecommunication Engineering

Product Showcase, also known as Anveshan is one of the annual event held by IEEE and IETE committees of EXTC department. Showcasing products from major industries as well as local industries in the neighboring area. We host around 50 companies every year, displaying their products throughout our event. With a footfall of more than 750 students from across different streams, it is one of the most anticipated event organized by EXTC department. Products ranging from smart lights and elevator panels to humanoids and 3D printers, products from medical domain such as automated injections and a replica of a production line are displayed. Boasting some products from major companies like Siemens, Verma Brothers, Bosons machines, Fox Domotics etc. Anveshan is one of the important event which helps to introduce current trends in industry to the students and as well bridges the gap between the students and companies, helping students for their projects and future ventures.

MECH EXPO.

Department of Mechanical Engineering

Mech Expo is a technical exhibitory event conducted by the technical committees of Mechanical Engineering Department of Vidyavardhini's College of Engineering & Technology. It is one of the most informative event conducted in VCET which is received with a great deal of enthusiasm and engagement by the students as well as the faculty. Machinery, tools, casting dies, different smaller machine components and associated products are all showcased to provide a greater insight into the actual industrial products and their working. Such kind of an exposure enhances and refines the overall understanding of students and increases their inquisitiveness to emerging technology in the field of engineering.

ABACUS.

Department of Computer Engineering

Abacus, the technical exhibitory event of computer engineering department is one of the most prestigious event which takes place in Vidyavardhini's College of engineering and technology. Students come with their own set of technical skills and showcase their projects before the faculty members and fellow students. Every year, Abacus comes with its unique theme which reflects the modern day advancement in the computer field which attracts a huge number of students. Abacus aims to guide young engineers in such efficient manner that the innovators in them take life again. This year, Abacus has ventured into the product showcase which enhances students' knowledge about current trends in the computer world and introduces them to the real time industry related products. The event is judged by experienced industry professionals who encourage students with their remarkable and valuable feedback. Bringing in the products, helps the students to fill the technological gap between their projects and real time products.

ELIXIR.

Department of Information Technology

Elixir, the technical event of Information Technology department is one of the most prestigious event which takes place at Vidyavardhini's college of Engineering and Technology. It acquaintes the students with modern day technology and advancement in the IT field. Elixir conducts technical events like poster presentation, exploring open source softwares and it's uses. The event is judged by experienced faculties who encourage students with their remarkable feedback. Elixir provides a platform to encourage students to foster their technical skills by collaborating it with adequate use of their creative ideas.
