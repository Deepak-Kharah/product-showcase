Elixir, the technical event of Information Technology department is one of the most prestigious event which takes place at Vidhyavardhini's college of engineering and technology. It acquaintes the students with modern day technology and advancement in the IT field. Elixir conducts technical events like  poster presentation, exploring open source softwares and it's uses. The event is judged by experienced faculties who encourage students with their remarkable feedback. Elixir provides a platform to encourage students to foster their technical skills by collaborating it with adequate use of their creative ideas.
