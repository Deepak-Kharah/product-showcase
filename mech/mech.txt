Mech Expo is a technical exhibitory event conducted by the technical committees of Mechanical Engineering Department of Vidyavardhini's College of Engineering & Technology. It is one of the most informative event conducted in VCET which is received with a great deal of enthusiasm and engagement by the students as well as the faculty. Machinery, tools, casting dies, different smaller machine components and associated products are all showcased to provide a greater insight into the actual industrial products and their working. Such kind of an exposure enhances and refines the overall understanding of students and increases their inquisitiveness to emerging technology in the field of engineering.
